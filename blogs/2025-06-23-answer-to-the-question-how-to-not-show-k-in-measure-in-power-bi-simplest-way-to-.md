If you wish to show all the numbers of a measure in card visual in Power BI without K or M do the following- Step 1- Select the card Step 2- Go to style Step 3- in style "don't select General" click on "Visual" Step 4 - Expand "call out value" Step5-In display units drop down select "none" Home
