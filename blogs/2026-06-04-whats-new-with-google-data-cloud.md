Google Cloud's Data Analytics, BI, and Database teams published a comprehensive roundup of recent product announcements and features. Highlights include Beyond the Query workshops (June 1-5) on NoSQL databases for AI agent development, a Bigtable free trial with a 1-node SSD cluster and up to 500GB storage, and a detailed timeline covering Managed Service for Apache Airflow 3.1, BigQuery Graph, Conversational Analytics for Looker, and Cloud SQL enhancements including autoscaling read pools and Microsoft Entra ID integration.
