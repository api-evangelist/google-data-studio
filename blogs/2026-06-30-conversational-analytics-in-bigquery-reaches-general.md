Google announced general availability of Conversational Analytics in BigQuery, letting users query data and generate reports using natural language via Gemini models. It offers trusted agentic reasoning with visible thinking steps, context citations, proactive disambiguation, cross-cloud data analysis, and multi-step deep-dive investigations.
