Google Cloud rebranded Dataproc as Managed Service for Apache Spark and announced Next '26 updates for the managed clusters deployment mode. The post covers three core pillars: making Spark faster through a native execution engine, easier to run with smarter scaling policies that reduce operational overhead, and smarter by embedding Gemini-powered extensions directly into the development and operational lifecycle.
